Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What all does the fee include and not include?
    • The fee includes use of the premises, furniture, whiteboards, storage, drinking water, cleaning, electricity, power back-up and shared facilities such as washrooms.
    • Shared Counselling Areas and Faculty Waiting Areas during your class time is included (where applicable). If you want your counsellor / faculty member to be present on the premises outside of class hours, or on other days, those might be charged separately, depending on the SpacePartner.
    • It also includes the cost of a shared support staff member who who will help you with any furniture rearrangement, if required. Over-time charges for the shared staff member (if applicable) on a SpacePartner’s premises will be charged extra.
    • If you want a nanny on the premises to help your students (younger children) to go to the washroom, charges for a nanny’s services will be extra.
    • Projector charges and GST @18%, if charged by the SpacePartner, will be extra
  2. Will there be an agreement signed?
    • Yes, once your request has been approved to conduct the program, there will be a tri-party MOU (Click Here) between SpaceBoat, SpaceUser and SpacePartner
  3. Who sets the Fee? Is there any scope for negotiation?
    • Fee is set by the SpacePartner. SpaceBoat makes a recommendation to a SpacePartner at the time of tie-up, but the final fee chart is set by a SpacePartner.
    • A potential User has the option to send only one counter proposal based on their requirement. A SpacePartner can then accept or reject the counter proposal.
    • If SpacePartner rejects the counter proposal, SpaceUser will then have to accept the original fee. Second counters will not be entertained.
  4. How long can I reserve the space for? What will be the term/tenure?
    • For as long and short as you want, subject to minimum fee thresholds set by a SpacePartner.
    • For requirements up to 2 months - refer to our Workshop / Short Term Courses plan.
    • For requirements longer than 2 months, refer to Monthly plans. Term for monthly plans will be for up to 300 days (one annual academic cycle). You can request for a longer term, but that will be subject to approval from SpacePartner.
  5. Can I rearrange furniture as per my course requirement? Who will help me set up? Will there be someone on the premises while my classes are going on?
    • Yes, you can. A shared support staff member will be present on the premises to help you with any furniture rearrangement.
    • This is a shared support staff member who might be helping other SpaceUsers on the premises help set up as well. Please be patient
    • This shared support staff member is NOT responsible for the safety of your students or to supervise them in case your students (young children) need to go to the washroom. If you need a nanny for these purposes, charges will be extra.
    • Additionally, a SpaceBoat representative will be present on the premises to help you onboard during the initial days. After that, SpaceBoat representatives will be present on an as-requested basis for any troubleshooting / grievance redressal.
  6. Can I bring my own furniture?
    • If the furniture that you require does not exist at the SpacePartner’s premises, you can bring your own additional furniture, subject to a SpacePartner’s approval
  7. I will need internet that can accommodate multiple logins simultaneously.
    • You will have to secure your own internet as per your requirement.
  8. I will need to use the SpacePartner’s venue as address in my communication. Can I start marketing of the batch before reserving the space?
    • You can use the SpacePartner’s address as venue location in your marketing collaterals only after reserving the space.
    • We strongly recommend that you reserve the space before beginning marketing of the program. If you haven’t reserved the space and end up forming a batch, you might be risking your revenue and reputation if the SpacePartner denies the request to host your program.
  9. What if I am not able to form my batches after reserving the space? What if I get more students than anticipated?
    • If you do not get the desired response to your marketing efforts, don’t worry -
      • You can claim a full refund of the security deposit and the monthly fee, if you inform us up to 5 days in advance of the start date.
      • You can claim a full refund of the security deposit and the monthly fee, if you inform us up to 5 days in advance of the start date.
    • If you get an overwhelmingly positive response to your marketing efforts, you can increase the number of hours or switch to a higher capacity room, subject to availability, and pay as per the applicable charges.
  10. I want to conduct a few trial classes before I commit to beginning operations from the premises. Is that possible?
    • You can reserve the space for Trial classes under the Workshop Plans and pay an amount accordingly. And a separate amount for your recurring monthly classes as per the monthly plans.
    • After your trial classes, if you choose not to continue, the amount and the security deposit for the monthly recurring classes will be refunded to you
    • We strongly recommend that you reserve the space for both the trial period and the recurring monthly course separately. In case your trial classes result in enrolments, you do not want the headache of having to look for alternate venue in case your request for monthly classes is denied subsequently.
  11. Can I change my requirement in subsequent months, after I have started?
    • Yes you can. The SpaceBoat solution offers you complete flexibility. You can change to a larger / smaller capacity room, increase / decrease number of hours in the Beginner Plan or switch to a higher / lower plan, subject to required inventory being available.
  12. Will I get a counselling desk at the SpacePartner’s premises?
    • You will be provided a shared Counselling Desk / area during your class timings, without any extra charge.
    • Alternatively, you can reserve a dedicated counselling room for extra charges, depending on such a room being available on a SpacePartner’s premises.
  13. Can my faculty come to the premises earlier? Can there be a workspace allocated for my faculty?
    • Your faculty will be allowed in the premises up to 15 minutes before the class, and can stay until 15 minutes after the class.
    • A few SpacePartners do have a workspaces for faculty members to work from, beyond class timings. Such workspaces will be charged separately.
  14. Will there be a waiting area for parents?
    • Most SpacePartners on SpaceBoat have a strict ‘drop-off’ policy on the premises. A parent is required to drop off the child and leave the premises, unless they are required to be present in the class with the child (as in the case of mother-toddler programs).
    • In cases where SpacePartners allow parents to wait on the premises, there will be a common designated waiting area allocated for the same. However, this is a shared facility and seating will be subject to availability.
  15. How will I do my branding?
    • The following support is provided as part of the standard solution across all plans:
      • Venue Address - You will be able to use the SpacePartner’s name & location as venue address in your communication.
      • Standees during class timings - You can put up standees in a high footfall area in the premises, while your class is going on.
    • In addition, users with a billing in excess of Rs. 12,500/mo at a location can put their logos on a common display board:
      • Such common display board will be placed within the premises - along the passageway, or at the reception.
      • A SpacePartner reserves the right to refuse the logo being displayed on the common board outside.
      • There will be one-time set-up charges for this.
      • It will be a common display with logos of all other Users running their courses from the premises. For sample, click Here
  16. Who will answer the leads/walk-ins during our non-reserved hours? Who will collect the money from the students?
    • In case of a walk-in by a student outside your class hours, or a call received at the reception, the details of the lead will be noted down and will be transferred to you.
    • You will be responsible for fee collection from your students.
  17. What all marketing support will I get? Can I do promotional activities for the students of the SpacePartner? Will SpaceBoat help me get more students?
    • SpaceBoat is a primarily a space-sharing solution. Very few SpacePartners offer a little bit of marketing support including the following -
    • Mailers to parents regarding evening activities.
    • Canopy/stalls on PTMs, carnivals etc.
    • A SpacePartner reserves the right to refuse any marketing support to a SpaceUser. Such permission will be accepted or denied by a SpacePartner at the time of approving a SpaceUser’s program to operate from the premises.
    • Please note, SpaceBoat does not undertake any service level accountability for marketing support by a SpacePartner. The quality of service and resultant outcome is outside the scope of SpaceBoat’s operations.
    • If you use the SpacePartner’s resources for your own marketing efforts before you begin your program, security deposit and the advance fee will not be refundable.
    • SpaceBoat is experimenting with separate avenues to help a SpaceUser with their marketing. This service is currently in beta.
    • For further details, read the Marketing Support Servies (beta)
  18. What about other competing programs? Will they be allowed to operate from the premises?
    • SpaceBoat is open to holding discussions with any competing program until a SpaceUser reserves the place
    • Once a SpaceUser reserves the premises, SpaceBoat will not entertain any requests from a competing program during this period.
    • However, SpaceBoat may explore the possibility of a competing program provider offering different courses than the ones being provided by the SpaceUser.
      • Eg 1 - if a Dance provider is conducting Hip-Hop and Bollywood classes, SpaceBoat may invite partners offering Ballet, Salsa or Classical forms.
      • Eg 2 - if a provider is conducting German Language Classes, SpaceBoat may invite partners offering Spanish, French classes, etc
      • Eg 3 - if a provider is conducting digital marketing course, SpaceBoat may invite partners offering courses in Analytics, Coding, etc
